Why We Started:

Our journey began with a shared passion for storytelling through film. We wanted to create a space where movie lovers from all walks of life could come together to explore, discuss, and celebrate the world of cinema. The lack of a dedicated platform for this purpose inspired us to create MEGMA Movie.
